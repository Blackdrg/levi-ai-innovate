Privacy Sanctuary

Last Manifestation: March 22, 2026

LEVI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our AI-powered wisdom guide.

1. Data We Collect

Personal Identity: When you register or use Google OAuth, we collect your email address and profile name. This is used for account management and personalized content delivery.

Usage Data: We store your chat history, generated quotes, and artistic creations (images/videos) to provide a persistent "Studio" experience and allow you to revisit your journey.

2. Data Processors

To provide our services, we share limited data with the following processors:

  • Groq Cloud: Processes your chat messages to generate philosophical responses.
  • Together AI: Receives quote text to generate background artistic visualizations.
  • Resend: Handles the delivery of daily wisdom and transactional emails.
  • Razorpay: Processes all payment transactions securely. We do not store your credit card details.
  • AWS S3: Stores your generated images and videos.

3. Data Retention & Your Rights

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can download a full export of your data from your Profile page.
  • Erasure: You can delete your account and all associated personal data at any time via the Settings menu.
  • Rectification: You can update your profile information in the Studio.

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data in transit and follow best practices for securing data at rest. Access to your personal data is strictly limited to the core LEVI engine for service delivery.

5. Communing

Should you seek further clarity on these principles, reach out to the architects at support@levi-ai-create.com.
