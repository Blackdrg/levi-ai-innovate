Frequently Asked

What is a credit?

1 credit generates one AI image (1024×1024). Video card generation costs 3 credits. AI chat does not consume credits.

When do credits reset?

Credits reset on the 1st of each month. Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel your subscription anytime. You retain access until the end of your billing period.
